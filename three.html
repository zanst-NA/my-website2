Catering

 

When we cater for our restaurant, we handle food and service for events outside the main dining area. It involves:

  1. Planning the Menu: We work with clients to customize dishes for their event, like weddings or parties.

  2. Preparing the Food: We cook meals either in advance or on-site, depending on the setup.

  3. Delivering the Food: We make sure everything arrives safely and on time.

  4. Providing Service Staff: We might bring waitstaff, bartenders, or set up a buffet.

  5. Supplying Equipment: We ensure the event has everything needed—plates, utensils, tables, and even decorations.

  6. Customizing to Client Needs: We accommodate dietary restrictions and special preferences.

It’s a great way to bring the restaurant’s quality to private events and boost revenue.
